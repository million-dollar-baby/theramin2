About

TIMES is an initiative to provide quality media education that is aimed at craeting employment opportunities among the students who want to carve a path in TV, web and film industry. The idea behind the institute is to create a culture among students. Culture of learning an art from that is commercially visible in the everexpanding media super-bazar.
