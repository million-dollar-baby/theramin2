About

Quality Media education is need of the hour. In India, where the veracity of information is fast becoming elusive, there is still scant conversation about structured media literacy modules at the school level. TIMES is an initiative to provide quality media education at reasonable rates . The courses are aimed at creating employment opportunities among the youth who want to carve a path for themselves in television, web and film industry. The core idea behind the institute is to create a culture of learning an art form that is commercially viable in the ever-expanding media super bazar. Study under the guidance of top notch professionals and dedicated mentors. Lessons learnt at TIMES will leave you fully equipped and will help you find work in an industry that is competitive, challenging and rewarding.

Embark on a wonderful Journey in Media Industry.

Infrastructure

The institute has a well-designed, spacious and acoustically treated classroom. It has two in-house broadcast quality sound studios for hands on experience and audio/video production facility for internship and placements. Students have full access to experience the professional work environment inside the studios during their course.

Classroom

TIMES features an aesthetically designed classroom for lectures and screenings. It is equipped with 12 independent workstations having iMac's for students for learning and practise. An HD projector is also provided for film screenings and workshops. The classroom also serves as a venue for TIMES FRIDAY JAM SESSIONS for students.

Studio Red

Studio Red is our flagship Studio. With its superior design and professional acoustic treatment, its an excellent space for students to learn the art of sound designing, recording, 5.1 mixing and mastering. It is equipped with analog gear, high-end reference audio speakers and microphones.

Studio Blue

Studio Blue is a boutique studio boasting fantastic ambiance and work atmosphere. Its compact strategic design makes learning much more fun. Dubbing room and microphone selection makes it possible for students to record with great variety in hand. It undertakes indie and commercial soundtrack production, voice-overs, dubs and stereo mixing.

Academy Director




Sibtain Shahidi

Mr Sibtain Shahidi is an academician, author, screen writer, film educator and documentry film maker. He has got extensive experience of teaching in media institutes like Whistling Woods International, ZIMA and others.
His written stories are published in Indian Academy Of Letters, Govt. of India. He conducts workshops on cinema and its language, in universities and colleges aroud the country.
